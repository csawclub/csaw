What we're about.

It is the mission of the Whitman College Student Activities Club to promote awareness and foster interest in Computer Science and programming within the Whitman community, and to foster skills and relationships.

What we do.

Meaningful real world applications through projects, workshops, hackathons, and other events and activities related to Computer Science and technology.

Where you come in.

We are looking for students of all backgrounds, interests, and disciplines to join us in developing modern technology to solve problems for all kinds of issues.
